Instructions

This tool creates cards for the edition app, some rules: headlines must be sort, they can't be more than two lines, use the size tool to help up you. Images should be cropped in the grid to your specific requirments, use the media link and paste it into the immage url. Once you have created your card you can download the image and reupload to the grid ready for use in the editon.
